About Gear Guide

When was the last time you asked a chatbot for help and got a useful, contextual answer? If you're like most users, the experience probably felt disjointed repetitive replies, no memory of past questions, and vague, unhelpful instructions.

Gear Guide was born from that frustration. It’s an AI-powered advisor designed to troubleshoot vehicle issues with the clarity, structure, and depth of a seasoned mechanic. Unlike traditional bots, Gear Guide understands your context, remembers your questions, and delivers structured, actionable diagnostics.

How It Works

  • Powered by a Neo4j knowledge graph of components, problems, symptoms, procedures, and more
  • Uses OpenAI embeddings to semantically understand your query
  • Combines dense (vector) and sparse (keyword) search for hybrid relevance
  • Automatically extracts structured data from car manuals via GPT no manual tagging required
  • Maintains multi-turn conversation context to ensure continuity

What Sets It Apart

Gear Guide doesn’t just find relevant text it thinks diagnostically. It connects symptoms to problems, recommends test procedures, and identifies likely faulty components based on real-world reasoning paths.

It’s like having a mechanic’s brain, backed by a graph database and an LLM, available 24/7.

Future Vision

  • Expand to domains like home appliances, electronics, and HVAC systems
  • Integrate with voice assistants and mobile interfaces for real-time diagnostics
  • Continuous learning from technician feedback and logs
  • Open SDK for developers to build their own RAG-powered advisors

We’re redefining what it means to "ask for help." Welcome to the future of diagnostics. Welcome to Gear Guide.
